Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Go back to HOMEPAGE

Here is a great recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies!

This is a very easy recipe for beginners since it requires very little ingredients!

Hope you like it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional:3/4 cups chopped nuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Whisk the flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Mix the butter and sugars together until smooth and creamy. Beat in the egg and vanilla.
  4. Slowly beat the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until no flour pockets remain. Do not overmix. Fold in the nuts and chocolate, if using.
  5. Use a tablespoon to shape dough into balls and place on greased cooking sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool completely in the pan set on a wire rack.
